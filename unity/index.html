Unity 3D for Gaming

It was an excellent opportunity to discover the world of object-oriented programming, game design and development. I produced a presentation available HERE (data from High School projects) in which I presented all the work I did showing also the main features of Unity 3D and explaining (text in Italian) pieces of code I produced in C#.
